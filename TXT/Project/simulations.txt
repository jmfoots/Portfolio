~Three Bit Computer Simulation|Fall 2019|https://jmfoots.github.io/Portfolio/PROJECTS/TBC/3BitComputer.pdf|IMG/tbc.png|
Click image to view circuit diagram PDF.
Created a three bit computer in Logisim Evolution that performs bit-wise operations.
Performed NOT, AND, NAND, OR, XOR, NOR, XNOR, and ADD operations.
Used three Read/Write Registers and three Read-Only Registers.
Accepted input to two registers and places output in selected register.
