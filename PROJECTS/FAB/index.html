Faith Academy Baseball

It is the mission of Faith Baseball to first and foremost exalt the name of Jesus Christ through baseball. In doing this the Faith Baseball team will bring respectability as Christians to athletics. Young men will also be given the opportunity to play baseball at the highest level they dare to dream and are willing to commit to.

Our goal is to live the name "Faith".
